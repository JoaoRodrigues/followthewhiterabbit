Join us in Portugal!

We will be marrying in Lousã, a beautiful village in the center of Portugal where João spent a lot of his childhood. We want to celebrate this special day surrounded by friends and family, so we hope this page helps you find all the information you need to plan your trip!

The Venue

The wedding ceremony and reception will both take place at the Palácio da Lousã, an 18th century mansion turned boutique hotel in the center of the village, with views of the mountains and their schist villages. The Palácio, including the gardens, spa, and indoor and outdoor pools, will be exclusively ours from Friday the 11th to Sunday the 13th, so we highly encourage you to stay for the whole weekend! Map

Travel & Lodging

You will want to fly in to either Lisboa (LIS) or Porto (OPO); Lousã is roughly equidistant from either airport. Lisboa will probably have cheaper flights if you're flying from outside Europe, while Porto might be better for European low-cost carriers.

Then, to get to Lousã, we strongly recommend you rent a car at the airport. While there is public transport to Lousã (train + bus), it's not a straightforward route if you don't speak Portuguese. Also, you will be better off driving if you want to explore the region!

We reserved all of the venue's rooms for our guests. If you wish to stay with us, we ask you to stay for a minimum of two nights (215€/night). Guests staying for the full weekend (3 nights) will have a reduced rate (170€/night). Please use the RSVP form below for booking, to help us maximize the limited number of beds!

In addition to the Palácio, Lousã has a variety of accomodations at various price points, including the very nice Casa das Condessinhas a few houses down the street from the venue.
If you have any questions or need help with travel plans, don't hesitate to call or message either of us!
